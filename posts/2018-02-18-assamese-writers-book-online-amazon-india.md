#### Lakshminath Bezbaroa&#8217;s Books

The name of Lakshminath Bezbaroa reminds me of my childhood who wrote so many children&#8217;s literature. Sahityarathi Laksminath Bezbaroa was the pioneer of modern Assamese literature who wrote short stories, plays, poet, novel and more. Moreover, **O Mur Apunar Dex**, a patriotic song composed by him, is the state anthem of Assam. She was honored with  Sahitya Akademi Award (1983), the Jnanpith Award (2001) and Principal Prince Claus Laureate (2008) for her literary works. Mamoni Raisom aka Indira Goswami was an eminent writer, a prolific author, a Ramayana scholar, an editor and former Professor of Delhi University.
